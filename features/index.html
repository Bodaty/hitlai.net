Control

Trust journey, per workflow.

Each part of your business moves at its own pace. AI watches, suggests, executes-with-approval, or handles routine while your team handles exceptions. Built-in approval workflows, review queues, escalation paths.

  • Phase 1: AI watches — insight, zero risk
  • Phase 2: AI suggests, your team decides
  • Phase 3: AI acts, you approve (one-click in Slack)
  • Phase 4: AI handles routine, your team handles exceptions
  • Governance attaches at workflow creation, not just runtime
Govern

Every AI action checked before it runs.

Before an agent sends an email, charges a card, or changes a record, the action is checked against your rules — the moment it happens, not after the fact. Allow it, hold it for one-click approval, or block it, with a full record of every decision.

  • Action-level checks on every agent — the ones you set up and any you connect
  • Set a rule once; it applies across every workflow and channel
  • Real-time approvals — one click in Slack, email, or the dashboard
  • Auto-allow the routine, stop or escalate the risky
  • Complete audit trail: every action, who approved it, and when
