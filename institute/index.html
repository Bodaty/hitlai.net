Questions we get a lot

How do I get my 3 free months of HitLai?

We activate it together in the final working session: you create your account at app.hitlai.net with your cohort code, your 90-day Business Starter trial applies automatically, and you can put a card on file — $0 that day, first charge only when the free months end, cancel anytime before then. Prefer a head start? The code in your seat-confirmation email works from the moment you receive it.

Do I need to be technical to attend?

No. These modules are built for SMB owners, ops leads, office managers, and department heads. If you can use a browser and an email client, you can set up the flows. A HitLai expert is in the room (or on the call) to help.

Do I need to subscribe to HitLai to attend?

No. Every attendee gets sandbox access during and after the session. Whether you subscribe is based on whether the ROI math works for your business — and we'll help you calculate that honestly, even if the answer is "not yet."

How is this different from Trailhead, HubSpot Academy, or Coursera?

Those teach one vendor's product (lock-in) or pure theory (no implementation). We teach with any AI (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, local Ollama) and you walk out with one real flow running in your business — AI, your team, and your existing systems coordinated. Knowledge that ships, not knowledge that decays.

What about my data — is anything leaving my network?

Your choice. Run on our managed cloud or inside your own network — including air-gapped, with local AI via Ollama or vLLM. Your data is not training anyone's model. For regulated industries we run vertical tracks (ABA 512 for legal, OCC/FDIC for banking, HIPAA for healthcare) — see the industries page.

Is this available outside Chicago?

In-person sessions are Chicago-only for the Phase 1 pilot. Online live and recorded modules are available everywhere. If you want us to run an in-person session in your city, tell us in the signup form — we route by demand.

Will there be a certification?

Phase 1 offers completion badges (digital, LinkedIn-displayable) at no extra cost. Formal AICtrlNet Certified Practitioner / Specialist / Architect exams launch later in 2026. See aictrlnet.com/certified when live.

Can I bring a colleague?

Yes, and it helps you. In-person attendees who bring a colleague get an additional month free on their HitLai subscription. Two people from the same business tend to implement — one person tends to plan.

What if my company needs an enterprise program?

AICtrlNet Institute runs parallel programs for enterprise architects, CTOs, and compliance leaders. Contact enterprise@bodaty.com.
