The trust journey

Six phases. Start where you’re comfortable.

Phase 1–4 are the buyer-facing trust journey. Phase 5–6 extend into enterprise autonomy with structured oversight.

Phase 1

AI watches.

“Show me.”

  • AI shows insights
  • Your team does the work
  • See value with zero risk
  • Learn your workflows
Autonomy
Phase 2

AI suggests.

“Suggest to me.”

  • AI recommends
  • Your team decides
  • Confidence builds with use
  • Trust grows over time
Autonomy
Phase 3

AI acts, you approve.

“Do it, I’ll approve.”

  • AI executes; your team signs off
  • One-click approvals in Slack
  • Approval workflows built in
  • Full oversight maintained
Autonomy
Phase 4

AI handles routine, your team handles exceptions.

“Do it, ask sometimes.”

  • AI runs the pattern
  • Your team owns the edge cases
  • Smart escalation on novel cases
  • Audited end to end
Autonomy
Phase 5

AI anticipates.

“Anticipate my needs.”

  • AI anticipates needs
  • Proactive suggestions
  • Pattern recognition
  • Strategic recommendations
Autonomy
Phase 6

Autopilot.

“Run my company.”

  • Full AI operations
  • Exception-based involvement
  • Strategic oversight only
  • Coordinated AI organization, ready in minutes
